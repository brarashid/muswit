Who We Are

More Than an NGO — A Sisterhood

MUSWIT (Muslim Women in Teaching) was born from a simple but powerful belief: that educating and empowering a woman transforms not just her life, but her entire community and the generations that follow.

Founded by educators, advocates, and community leaders, MUSWIT bridges the gap between aspiration and opportunity for women and girls across our region — delivering programs in education, skills development, ICT, and civic empowerment.

We are not just an organization. We are a movement driven by love, purpose, and an unshakeable commitment to women's potential.

Our Mission

To empower Muslim women and girls through quality education, skills development, civic engagement, and community programs — enabling them to become leaders, entrepreneurs, and change-makers in their societies.

Our Vision

A world where every Muslim woman and girl has equal access to education, opportunity, and the freedom to live her fullest life — contributing her unique gifts to a stronger, more equitable society.

Core Values

The Principles We Live By

Our work is anchored in values that guide every decision, every program, and every relationship we build.

Empowerment

We believe every woman possesses untapped strength. Our programs are designed to unlock and amplify that innate power.

Community

Real change begins in communities. We work at the grassroots level, with communities, not just for them.

Equality

We fight for equal access to education and opportunity — regardless of economic background, geography, or circumstance.

Sustainability

Our programs are designed to create lasting change — equipping women with skills that generate income and opportunity for life.

Integrity

We hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards of transparency, responsibility, and ethical conduct in all we do.

Compassion

Every woman who walks through our doors is met with dignity, warmth, and the unwavering belief that she matters.

What We Do

Programs That Transform Lives

Our work spans four core pillars, each designed to address critical barriers women face on their path to empowerment.

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, tutoring, and mentorship to ensure girls stay in school and achieve academic excellence.

Vocational Training (TVET)

Practical skills in tailoring, catering, cosmetology, agriculture, and more — enabling women to generate sustainable income.

ICT & Digital Skills

Digital literacy, coding, graphic design, and digital entrepreneurship training for the 21st-century economy.

Community Outreach

Health education, civic engagement, leadership training, and awareness campaigns transforming entire communities.

How We Work

A Holistic Approach to Change

We don't just deliver programs — we build ecosystems of support that address the whole woman and her whole community.

Identify & Reach

We identify underserved women and girls through community networks, school partnerships, and local referrals.

Assess & Enroll

Each beneficiary is assessed to match her to the program that best fits her needs, goals, and circumstances.

Train & Empower

Women receive quality training from expert facilitators in safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environments.

Support & Launch

Graduates receive post-training mentorship, market access, and seed support to launch careers or businesses.

Why It Matters

When Women Rise, Entire Societies Rise With Them

Research consistently shows that investing in women's education generates some of the highest returns in development. Educated women invest 90% of their income back into their families. Empowered women raise healthier, more educated children.

At MUSWIT, we don't just see the woman in front of us — we see the ripple effect that radiates from her transformation into her family, her community, and the next generation. That's why this work matters. That's why we cannot stop.
