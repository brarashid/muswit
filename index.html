Who We Are

A Movement Committed to Empowering Women Through Education and Opportunity

MUSWIT — Muslim Women in Teaching — is a non-governmental organization dedicated to transforming the lives of women and girls through targeted education, vocational empowerment, and civic engagement. We believe every woman holds the potential to reshape her community.

From classrooms to boardrooms, we walk alongside women at every stage of their journey — providing skills, support, and a sisterhood that stands strong.

What We Do

Programs That Change Lives

We deliver targeted interventions that address the real barriers women face — from the classroom to the community.

Education Support

Scholarships, tutoring, mentorship, and resources to ensure every girl completes her education and reaches her full potential.

Vocational Training (TVET)

Hands-on technical and vocational programs that equip women with market-relevant skills for sustainable livelihoods.

ICT & Digital Skills

Bridging the digital divide through technology literacy programs, coding bootcamps, and digital entrepreneurship training.

Community Outreach

Grassroots campaigns, health education, and civic engagement programs that transform entire communities from within.

Civic & Diplomatic Relations

Building Bridges Across Governments & Nations

MUSWIT actively engages with government bodies, embassies, and international organizations to advocate for women's rights and community development at the highest levels.

Our diplomatic relationships translate into real policy change, funding, and opportunities for the women we serve.
